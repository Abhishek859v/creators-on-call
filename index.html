The Formula for Growth

Your 3-Day COC Itinerary

(Example based on our **Rishikesh** retreat—activities adjust to location, but the results stay the same.)

1

Day 1: Connection, Content, & Challenge

Focus: Breaking the Ice and Collaborative Production

  • **Arrival & Vibe Check:** Check-in, welcome kit, and a casual lunch to meet the cohort.
  • **Vision Boarding:** Set the tone and share your niche, potential, and biggest content achievement.
  • **The Location Reel Challenge:** Teams dive into their first challenge: a 60-second collaborative reel capturing the location's essence and introducing the "COC" concept.
  • **Bonfire Networking:** Themed dinner, followed by a bonfire and fun games (like 'Never Have I Ever') to forge deep, relaxed bonds.
2

Day 2: Skill Mastery & The Viral Strategy

Focus: Peer-to-Peer Learning and High-Impact Content

  • **The Skill-Share Session:** Pair up with a creator from a different niche (e.g., editor + writer) to shoot a high-value "How-To" video, forcing hands-on cross-skill collaboration.
  • **Content Production Block:** Dedicated time with technical crew support (lighting, angles) to film the collaborative content.
  • **The 100K Views Task:** The signature challenge! Teams create a video with a clear strategy for reaching 100,000 views, pushing the limits of their viral knowledge.
  • **Community Feedback Lab:** Present and critique all content and viral ideas. Constructive feedback is mandatory—everyone elevates each other.
3

Day 3: Showcase, Graduation, & Next Steps

Focus: Launching Content and COC Community Onboarding

  • **The Final Showcase:** Present the best-edited final content. The winning team gets a feature on the official COC channel!
  • **Closing Ceremony:** Receive your official COC Certificate to add credibility to your portfolio.
  • **The Path Forward:** Final speech on community steps, future collaborations, and joining the private COC network.
  • **Group Photo & Departure.**

Quick Answers

Your Questions, Answered

Am I eligible for a COC Invite?

COC is generally for **micro-creators (under 100K followers)** who are serious about monetizing and collaborating. We prioritize commitment and quality content over sheer follower count. If you are actively creating, you should apply!

What is included in the trip cost?

The cost covers **3 days/2 nights lodging** in the retreat villa, all **meals**, organized **activities/challenges**, and **on-site professional tech support**. It does **NOT** include your travel cost to and from the specified retreat location (e.g., Lonavla train station or Rishikesh airport).

What happens after I submit my invite request?

Our team reviews your profile within 48 hours. If approved, you will receive a direct email with a link to view available trip dates and process your secure booking and payment.

Do I need to bring my own equipment?

Yes, please bring your primary content capture tool (phone, mirrorless camera, etc.). We provide shared lighting, microphones, and basic collaboration props for the challenges.
